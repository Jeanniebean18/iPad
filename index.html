Small yet powerful.


The new iPad mini 4 has a gorgeous 7.9-inch Retina display, yet it's small enough to hold in one hand. It has a powerful A8 chip with 64-bit desktop-class architecture, advanced iSight and FaceTime HD cameras, Wi-Fi and LTE connectivity, iCloud, the breakthrough Touch ID fingerprint sensor, and up to 10 hours of battery life.


Smooth and seamless.

With amazing apps in the App Store made just for iPad, you can do the things you love like never before. iPad mini 4 is powered by an A8 chip with second-generation 64-bit desktop-class architecture. Whether you’re browsing your photos, editing a video, or giving a presentation, your experience will be smooth and seamless.

A better experience with iOS 9

iPad mini 4 runs iOS 9, the most intuitive, advanced and secure mobile operating system in the world. If it feels like it was made for iPad, that’s because it was. With major improvements to an already essential set of built-in apps, smarter Siri and search features, and much more, iOS 9 is an experience like no other — on a device like no other.

Thin is an
understatement

iPad mini 4 combines power and portability with a thin 6.1mm design weighing just 0.65 pounds. To say it is thin is an understatement.

The iPad mini Smart Cover

The newly designed Smart Cover for iPad mini 4 is constructed from a single piece of polyurethane to protect the front of your device. The Smart Cover automatically wakes your iPad when opened and puts it to sleep when closed. It folds into different positions to create a stand for reading, viewing, typing, or making FaceTime calls. And it’s easy to remove, so you can snap it on and off whenever you want.
